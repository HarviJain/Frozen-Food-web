Who We Are

From Indian Farms to Global Markets

Abhyuday Bharat Food Cluster LLP is a leading Project Management Consultant delivering world-class agricultural infrastructure, food processing facilities, and farm-to-market solutions across India. Backed by a cooperative network of 7,000+ PACS and 40+ lakh farmers across Gujarat, we deliver consistent, traceable, export-ready frozen foods to global markets.

Through our Co-operative Private Partnership (CPP) model, we connect farmers, cooperatives, processors and global buyers in an integrated, sustainable value chain — supplying GCC, Europe, Canada, Australia, Russia and beyond.
